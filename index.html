About

My name is Saara Vällik and I live in Tartu, Estonia. I am studying at Tartu Vocational Training Center to become Software Developer (Web Spetsialist) and my goal right now is to find an internship opportunity where I can take my studies even further.

Little bit more about me…

In my heart I’m a good and caring person who likes to think further than tip of my nose. Thinking about problems and finding solutions to them so the outcome is a better world for someone is something that drives me. I like to share my ideas, speak in front of people and organize events. I believe that always learning and self improving is on of the key elements to success.

I am teachable and I like to teach. If I don’t know how something works I’ll ask for help rather than give up or hide in shame of not knowing.

I can also say about myself that I’m a self-learner which I can prove with my quite uncommon hobby as well - I have been involved with dog training for 9 years and most of the skills I have learned through online classes, youtube and having fun experiments with my dogs to see how they learn and react. My dogs know tons of cool tricks and people love them - I have performed at kindergartens, orphanages and opening events with the goal to teach people more about dogs and how much fun you can have with them. But my main focus lays in the dog sport agility where I compete at the highest level, go to competitions and training regularly and teach other dog owners as well.

The thing I love the most is the fact that my active hobby perfectly balances my physically-not-so-active IT studies and the similarity of endless learning!
